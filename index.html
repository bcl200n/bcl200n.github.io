About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Tongji University, supervised by Prof. Menggang. My research integrates remote sensing, InSAR analysis, and AI-driven resilience modeling to study urban renewal and seismic adaptation in Southwest China. Previously, I served as Lecturer at Xichang University, and Visiting Scholar at Massey University (New Zealand).
