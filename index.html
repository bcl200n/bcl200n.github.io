Hi, I'm Chunlin Bian 👋

I’m a PhD candidate in Architecture at Tongji University. My work explores AI for the built environment—how foundation models, Earth‑observation, and digital twins can enhance seismic resilience and urban renewal. I build data‑to‑decision pipelines combining satellite/IoT sensing, vision‑language models, agent‑based simulation, and AIGC to support planning, design, and emergency management.

Selected Projects

I prototype AI‑augmented workflows from sensing → understanding → generation → decision, validated on real sites (e.g., Xichang Ancient City) for monitoring, iterative design, and resilience planning.

InSAR Time‑Series for Ground Deformation

Sentinel‑1A based monitoring of subsidence/uplift for early‑warning and resilient planning in Xichang.

VLM + AIGC for Urban Design

Using vision‑language models to parse multi‑source data and drive generative design alternatives in heritage contexts.

Digital Twin & Agent‑based Evacuation

City‑scale twin with multi‑agent simulation for evacuation and emergency response under earthquake scenarios.

O2O Cultural IP & Metaverse Ops

Data‑to‑experience pipeline linking virtual experiences to on‑site economy for heritage‑led urban renewal.
