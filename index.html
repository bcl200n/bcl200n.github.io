About

Hi! I’m Chunlin Bian 👋

I am a PhD candidate in Architecture at Tongji University, supervised by Prof. Menggang. My work explores how AI, Earth-observation/InSAR, and digital twins can enhance seismic resilience and urban renewal in the built environment.
